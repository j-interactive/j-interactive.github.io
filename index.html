Joe Griffiths

I'm an independent senior engineer and e-commerce architect based in Cheltenham, UK. I build and operate the backends and integrations behind high-volume retail: bespoke APIs, payments, search, email, and the links into warehouse and stock systems that make the rest work. I work with SOZO, my current focus is integration-heavy Shopify and bespoke platforms, and I'm increasingly building AI into production.

I've been building for the web since 1999 and working with SOZO since 2007, across bespoke PHP platforms, the AWS and Linux infrastructure that runs them, and the e-commerce on top. Over the last year I've moved into Shopify extensibility, embedded apps and custom Functions handling checkout and discount logic, and into AI automation.

My stack is PHP, JavaScript, SQL, Liquid, Python, AWS, and Linux. I work extensively with bespoke APIs across REST and GraphQL, and with third-party integrations, payments, search, stock and fulfilment, and email, and I'm building AI into both e-commerce workflows and my own tooling.

Before SOZO I freelanced as j-interactive (2003–2007), and started out at County Business Net as a senior developer and network administrator (1999–2003). BSc in Multimedia and Computing from the University of Gloucestershire.
